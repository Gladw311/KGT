Vision

To raise mature, fully equipped and rightly positioned Christlike saints as Gatekeepers over all creation.

Objectives

  1. To raise Christlike saints who have a strong intimate walk with God.
  2. To raise Christlike saints who reflect the genes of Christ.
  3. To raise Christlike saints who are matured, fully equipped and rightly positioned within their area / nation of calling.
  4. To raise Christlike saints who will act as Gatekeepers in all 7 professional Nations of calling.

Forums

  • Gatekeepers Community Church - Weekly fellowships to raise mature, fully equipped Christlike saints.
  • Youth Empowerment Centre - Forum for teens and youth empowerment.
  • Coaching Curriculum - Training program for industry movers and shakers.
  • Training Manuals/Study Guides - Manuals for training and raising saints as industry shapers.
  • Short Term Training Schools - Schools for raising industry shapers with entry-level to advanced courses.
  • Long Term Training Schools - Intensive training for principality saints in various areas of calling.
  • Books and Publications - Publications focusing on believers as Gatekeepers.
